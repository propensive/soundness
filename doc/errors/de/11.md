# `SN-de/11` — Spacing around commas

Raised by the Decorum compiler plugin when whitespace around a comma
violates the project's style.

## Cause

Decorum requires that commas be flush with the preceding token and
followed by exactly one space.

## Variants

### `SN-de/11.1` — No space before a comma

A space appeared between the previous token and the comma.

**Example.** `[↯SN-de/11.1] no space is permitted before a comma`

**Resolution.** Remove the space before the `,`.

### `SN-de/11.2` — Exactly one space after a comma

The character following the comma was not a single space (either
zero, more than one, or a non-space character without a permitted
alignment exception).

**Example.** `[↯SN-de/11.2] exactly one space is required after a comma`

**Resolution.** Use exactly one space after each `,`.
