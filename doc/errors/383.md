# `SN-383` — `CourierError`

Raised when Caduceus cannot send an email message.

## Cause

Caduceus surfaces this error when the underlying transport (SMTP, or
a configured MTA) refuses or fails to deliver a message. The
constructor records the `from`, `to`, and `subject` for diagnosis.

## Example message

```
[↯SN-383] unable to send email from sender@example.com to recipient@example.com with subject Hello
```

## Resolution

Inspect the cause chain for the transport-level error (authentication,
DNS, recipient policy). Confirm the addresses are well-formed and
the MTA is reachable. More specific reasons may be added in future.
